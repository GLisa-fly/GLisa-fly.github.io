FAQ

Does LongCanvas require an account?

No. LongCanvas works without account registration.

Does LongCanvas upload my photos?

No. LongCanvas stitches selected images locally on your iPhone and does not upload them to a LongCanvas server.

Why does LongCanvas use the photo picker?

The iOS photo picker lets you choose the exact screenshots or photos you want to stitch.

What if automatic stitching looks wrong?

Use manual adjustment to fine-tune each connection, or switch direction and stitch again if the images belong in a different layout.

Why does saving ask for Photos permission?

LongCanvas asks for add-only library access when you save the stitched PNG back to Photos.
