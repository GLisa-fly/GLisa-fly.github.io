Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 4, 2026

1. Overview

LongCanvas is an iPhone app for stitching screenshots and photos into a single image. The app is designed to process selected images locally on your device.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share personal data, photos, screenshots, filenames, analytics data, or identifiers through our own backend services.

Images remain on your device unless you choose to save or share the stitched result using iOS system features.

3. Photos Access

LongCanvas uses the iOS photo picker so you can choose images to stitch. The picker is controlled by iOS, and the app receives only the images you select.

LongCanvas may request permission to add the finished stitched image to your photo library when you tap Save.

4. Local Processing

Overlap detection, manual adjustment, preview, export, and PNG sharing are performed on the device. LongCanvas does not upload your selected images to a LongCanvas server for stitching.

5. Third-Party Services

LongCanvas does not include advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs. Apple platform services may process technical information necessary for App Store distribution, iOS permissions, diagnostics, and operating-system services.

6. Children's Privacy

LongCanvas is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own services.

7. Changes

We may update this policy as LongCanvas changes. The effective date above shows when this page was last updated.
