Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

1. Overview

Clear Blocks is a free iPhone block puzzle game. It is designed to work without an account, advertising SDK, analytics backend, or third-party tracking service.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data through our own servers. Clear Blocks does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, microphone, or camera access.

3. Local Game Data

Clear Blocks may store gameplay settings and progress locally on your device, such as sound preferences, vibration preferences, completed adventure levels, and unlocked level progress.

This information is used only to provide the game experience on your device. Uninstalling the app removes its app container subject to iOS behavior.

4. Diagnostics

The app may create local technical logs in its own app container to help verify startup behavior during development and support. These logs are not uploaded to our own servers by Clear Blocks.

Apple may process crash reports, diagnostics, App Store download information, and device-level information according to Apple's own policies and your device settings.

5. Children

Clear Blocks is a general-audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own services.

6. Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date above shows when this page was last updated.
