Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

1. Overview

TidyClean is an iPhone app that helps people review similar photos, compress large videos, and merge duplicate contacts. We designed the app around a local-first workflow so that users can review cleanup suggestions before anything changes.

2. Data We Collect

We do not operate a backend that collects, stores, sells, or shares your photo library, videos, contacts, or cleanup history for our own analytics or advertising purposes.

Photo grouping, review signals, and contact merge preparation are intended to stay on your device unless you explicitly use a system share or export feature yourself.

3. Photos and Videos Permission

TidyClean requests access to your photo library so it can scan photo and video metadata, surface similar items, estimate storage savings, and perform the cleanup actions you explicitly confirm.

The app does not automatically delete your media. You review each suggested action before it is applied.

4. Contacts Permission

If you choose to use contact cleanup, TidyClean requests access to your contacts so it can identify likely duplicates and prepare merge suggestions for your review.

Contact changes are not applied until you confirm them.

5. Local Storage

The app may store settings, local indexes, review state, protected item markers, and cleanup history on your device so it can reopen recent work and provide a smoother review flow.

6. Purchases and Apple Services

If you buy a subscription or premium plan, Apple processes the purchase through the App Store. We do not receive your App Store payment credentials.

7. Third-Party Services

TidyClean is intended to function without our own customer-data backend. Standard Apple platform services may still process the technical information required to deliver iCloud Photos access, App Store purchases, and system-level sharing flows.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own services because the app is designed to work without account registration or our own content backend.

9. Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact bjlisa.a@gmail.com.

隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 4 月 22 日

1. 概述

TidyClean 是一款 iPhone 清理应用，帮助用户审阅相似照片、压缩大视频、 合并重复联系人。产品按本地优先思路设计，用户会先看到建议，再决定是否执行清理。

2. 我们收集的数据

我们不运营用于收集、存储、出售或共享你的照片库、视频、联系人或清理历史的自有后端， 也不会把这些内容用于自有广告或分析。

照片分组、审阅信号和联系人合并准备默认都在你的设备本地完成，除非你自己主动使用 系统分享或导出功能。

3. 照片与视频权限

TidyClean 会申请相册权限，用于扫描照片和视频元数据、识别相似内容、估算可释放空间， 并执行你明确确认过的清理动作。

应用不会自动删除媒体内容。每一步建议操作都需要你先审阅并确认。

4. 联系人权限

如果你选择使用联系人清理功能，TidyClean 会申请联系人权限，以识别疑似重复联系人并 生成供你审阅的合并建议。

联系人修改不会在你确认前自动写回。

5. 本地存储

应用可能会在设备本地保存设置、索引、审阅状态、保护项标记和清理历史，以便恢复最近工作 并提供更顺畅的清理体验。

6. 购买与 Apple 服务

如果你购买订阅或高级方案，交易会由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。 我们不会接收你的 App Store 支付凭据。

7. 第三方服务

TidyClean 设计目标是不依赖自有用户内容后端。Apple 平台服务仍可能处理访问 iCloud 照片、 App Store 购买和系统级分享流程所需的技术信息。

8. 未成年人隐私

由于应用设计为无需账号、无需自有内容后端，我们不会通过自有服务主动收集未成年人个人信息。

9. 联系方式

如有隐私相关问题，请发送邮件至 bjlisa.a@gmail.com。
