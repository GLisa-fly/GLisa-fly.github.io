FAQ

Does TidyHalo require an account?

No. TidyHalo is designed to work without account registration.

Will TidyHalo delete anything automatically?

No. You review and confirm each cleanup action before it is applied.

Why does the app ask for Photos access?

Photos access is required to scan your library, group similar items, estimate storage savings, and apply the actions you approve.

Why does the app ask for Contacts access?

Contacts access is only needed if you choose duplicate-contact cleanup so the app can prepare merge suggestions.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel them from the App Store subscription settings on your device.

常见问题

是否需要账号登录？

不需要，TidyHalo 设计为免账号使用。

会自动删除内容吗？

不会。每一步清理操作都需要你先审阅并确认。

为什么需要照片权限？

照片权限用于扫描图库、分组相似内容、估算可释放空间，并执行你明确批准的操作。

为什么需要联系人权限？

只有在你使用重复联系人整理时才需要，用于生成供你审阅的合并建议。

如何管理订阅？

订阅由 Apple ID 管理。你可以在设备上的 App Store 订阅设置中管理或取消。
