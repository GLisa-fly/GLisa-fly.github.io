FAQ

Does Voxlyn require an account?

No. Voxlyn is designed to work without account registration.

Does Voxlyn upload my speech to its own servers?

No. Voxlyn does not operate a backend that receives your conversation content.

Why does Voxlyn ask for microphone access?

Microphone access is required to capture speech during a live session.

Why do I need language packs?

Apple translation support may require both input and output language packs to be downloaded on your device before offline translation is available.

What should I do if translation is unavailable?

Open the language-pack screen in Voxlyn, confirm your selected pair, and follow the iOS download or Settings path shown for the required languages.
