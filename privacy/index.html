Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

1. Overview

OpenFly Interpreter is an iPhone app that performs speech transcription and translation primarily on-device. We designed the product to minimize data collection and avoid sending conversation content to our own servers.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data, voice recordings, transcriptions, translations, contact lists, or analytics data through our own backend services.

Conversation content stays on the device unless you explicitly export or share it yourself using system features provided by Apple.

3. Microphone and Speech Permissions

The app requests microphone access so it can listen to speech for live transcription. The app may also request access to Apple speech-related system features when available on your device to improve the local experience.

4. On-Device Processing and Optional Downloads

Speech recognition and translation are intended to run locally on the device after the required speech model files and Apple language packs are downloaded.

If you choose to download optional models or language resources, your device may connect to Apple or the selected model host in order to retrieve those files. We do not receive or retain the contents of those downloads.

5. Local Storage

The app may store settings, downloaded model references, and conversation history locally on your device so that the app can reopen recent sessions and work offline. You can remove locally stored sessions from within the app, and uninstalling the app removes its local app container subject to iOS behavior.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not operate a customer data backend for OpenFly Interpreter. Standard platform services from Apple and distribution endpoints for optional model downloads may still process technical network information necessary to deliver files, prevent abuse, or operate their services.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own services because the app is designed to function without a user account or our own data collection backend.

8. Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact bjlisa.a@gmail.com.

简体中文

隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 4 月 16 日

1. 概述

OpenFly Interpreter 是一款 iPhone 本地语音转写与翻译应用。我们尽量让语音识别 和翻译在设备本地完成，避免把对话内容发送到我们自建服务器。

2. 我们收集的数据

我们不会通过自有后端收集、存储、出售或共享你的个人信息、语音录音、转写内容、 翻译内容、通讯录信息或分析数据。

除非你主动使用系统分享或导出功能，否则对话内容会保留在你的设备本地。

3. 麦克风与语音权限

应用会申请麦克风权限，用于实时收听并进行本地语音转写。在你的设备支持时， 应用也可能调用 Apple 提供的本地语音相关能力以改善体验。

4. 本地处理与可选下载

在下载所需语音模型文件与 Apple 语言包后，语音识别和翻译会尽量在本机运行。

如果你主动下载可选模型或语言资源，设备可能会连接到 Apple 或所选模型资源提供方 以获取这些文件，但我们不会接收或保存这些下载内容。

5. 本地存储

应用可能会在设备本地保存设置、模型下载状态和最近会话，以便离线使用和恢复历史。 你可以在应用内删除本地会话，卸载应用后其本地容器会按照 iOS 规则被移除。

6. 第三方服务

我们不运营用于接收用户内容的后台服务。Apple 平台服务以及模型文件分发节点仍可能 为了提供下载、反滥用和基础服务运行而处理必要的技术网络信息。

7. 联系方式

如有隐私相关问题，请发送邮件至 bjlisa.a@gmail.com。
