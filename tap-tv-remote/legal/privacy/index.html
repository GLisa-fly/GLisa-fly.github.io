Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use this app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Local network information (e.g., IP addresses of devices on your home network) used solely to discover and connect to compatible televisions and smart devices. This information is processed on-device and not transmitted to our servers.
  • Device information such as TV brand, model identifiers, and connection type, stored locally to remember your configured devices.
  • App usage data such as button presses and feature interactions, collected in anonymized form for performance improvement purposes.
  • Device information such as OS version and app version for diagnostic purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Discover and connect to compatible TVs and smart devices on your local network.
  • Remember your configured devices for faster reconnection.
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and device compatibility.
  • Respond to support requests.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not sell your personal data. Network discovery data and device configuration are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers. Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained to improve app performance and expand device compatibility.

4. Local Network Access

This app requires access to your local Wi-Fi network solely to discover and communicate with compatible devices. We do not monitor your network traffic, access other devices beyond TV and smart device control, or transmit any local network data to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics or crash reporting. These services may collect anonymized usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not share personally identifiable information with third parties for advertising purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
