Summary: We Do Not Collect Any Data

NEON DROP is a relaxing cosmic color-sort puzzle game. It is 100% offline and completely private. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information or usage data whatsoever. There is no account, no login, and no server. Everything you do in the game stays on your device and never leaves it.

If you only read one section, this is it: we don't gather your data, because the app simply has no mechanism to do so.

Information We Collect

None.

NEON DROP does not collect any personal information or any data about you.

The only information the app stores is your game progress — such as your current level, coins earned by playing, power-ups, and the cosmic paintings you have unlocked in your collection. This information:

  • Is stored only on your device, using the standard operating-system preference store.
  • Is never sent to us, to any server, or to any third party.
  • Contains no personal identifiers and is not linked to your identity.
  • Is fully under your control and is removed when you delete the app (see "Data Storage & Deletion" below).

Information We Do NOT Collect

To be completely clear, NEON DROP does not access, request, or collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact details
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Your location (GPS or otherwise)
  • Your photos, camera, or microphone
  • Device identifiers, advertising identifiers (IDFA), or fingerprinting data
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Health, financial, or payment information
  • Any account, social-media, or login credentials
  • Any analytics, telemetry, or behavioral usage data

The app does not ask for any of these permissions and has no feature that would use them.

No Third Parties / No Ads / No Analytics / No Trackers

NEON DROP contains:

  • No advertising. There are no ads of any kind, and no advertising SDK.
  • No analytics. There is no analytics or telemetry SDK collecting how you play.
  • No third-party trackers. No social logins, no third-party SDKs that profile you.
  • No data sharing. Because no data is collected, there is nothing to share, sell, or disclose to anyone.

No Account or Login

NEON DROP does not have user accounts. You do not create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to play. You can enjoy the entire game anonymously.

Offline Operation

NEON DROP is designed to work entirely offline. The game does not require an internet connection to play, and it does not connect to any backend server during normal use. You can play with Wi-Fi and cellular data turned off, and the experience is identical.

Data Storage & Deletion

All of your game progress (level, coins, power-ups, and collected paintings) is stored locally on your device in the operating-system preference store. It is not backed up to our servers because we have none.

To delete this data, simply delete the app from your device. Removing NEON DROP removes all of its locally stored data. There is nothing further you need to do, and no request you need to send us, because we never had a copy of it.

Children's Privacy

NEON DROP is rated 4+ and is safe for players of all ages. It contains no violence, no profanity, no gambling or simulated gambling, no chat or social features, and no user-generated content.

Because the app collects no personal information from anyone — including children — it does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your region), and is consistent with the principles of the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar children's-privacy regulations. There is simply no data collection to consent to.

Security

Because NEON DROP stores data only on your own device and transmits nothing, there is no transfer of your information over the internet that could be intercepted, and no central database that could be breached. Your game data is protected by your device's own security and the operating system's standard app data protections. As with any software, no method of local storage can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure, but the app is built so that your information never leaves your device.

International Users

NEON DROP works the same way everywhere and does not transfer data across borders, because it does not transfer data at all. All processing happens locally on your device, regardless of where you are located. No personal data is moved to or stored in any other country.

If we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and post the revised version in the same location. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. The core commitment — that the app collects no data and operates offline — will continue to apply for as long as that remains true of the app.
