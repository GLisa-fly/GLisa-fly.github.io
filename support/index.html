FAQ

Does OpenFly Interpreter require an account?

No. The app is designed to work without account registration.

Does it work offline?

The main speech and translation workflow is designed to run on-device after required speech models and Apple language packs are downloaded.

Why does the app ask for microphone access?

Microphone access is required for live speech transcription.

How can I delete saved sessions?

Remove sessions from inside the app. Uninstalling the app also removes its local container subject to iOS behavior.

Does OpenFly upload my speech to your servers?

No. OpenFly Interpreter is designed so that speech recognition and translation happen on-device rather than through our own backend.

常见问题

是否需要账号登录？

不需要，应用设计为免账号使用。

是否支持离线使用？

在下载所需语音模型和 Apple 语言包后，核心流程会尽量在设备本地运行。

为什么需要麦克风权限？

实时语音转写依赖麦克风输入。

如何删除本地会话？

可在应用内删除历史会话；卸载应用也会移除其本地容器。

语音内容会上传到你们服务器吗？

不会。OpenFly Interpreter 设计目标就是尽量在本机完成识别与翻译。
